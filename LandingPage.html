About

About Us

Upon arrival you’ll immediately face a fridge with over 50 kinds of exclusive cold drinks. Anything from a Red Bull to Kingfisher Non Alcohol! Dining is done in boudoir-like seats on our entresol, where you’ll have a view on our open kitchen. We serve French-Asian cuisine with a “shared dining” concept. Meaning you can order one or multiple dishes and share them together. From ceviche and black truffle pasta to black cod marinated in miso. And don’t forget to try our special dish "Chicken" . Fan is very suitable for a cozy dinner or a walking dinner up to 60 people. Fully styled by Nikkie Plessen and OGER, our crew is looking forward to welcoming you!
